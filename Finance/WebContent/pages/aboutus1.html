About us

Trafalgar Capital Partners LLC (“TCP”) was established in Washington, DC in 2016. Its purpose is to provide investment banking and merchant banking services in the technology-enabled and business services industry with a particular focus on technology integration businesses, whether through offering strategic advice to firms, their shareholders and those interested in investing in the industry, or arranging equity investments in businesses we believe to be attractive. Since inception, TCP has advised the leading technology integration businesses in the commercial AV integration space.

Why We Are Different

Our sole focus is the technology integration sector, in particular commercial and pro-AV, with significant experience and knowledge in finding solutions for the specific issues that arise with regards to technology and people-based businesses.

The combination of both advisory and principal activities provides us with insights many other pure-play advisory firms lack; we believe being an investor makes us a better adviser, and vice versa. We understand the longer-term issues that may occur outside of a specific piece of execution work, and we can advise on preventing or planning for such potential problems accordingly.

We recognize the importance of long-term relationships with clients, and we understand that a more profound relationship that has been built over time means we can often provide better and more suited advice. Whilst transactional and execution projects are the major portion of our business, a significant part of our work is for clients with whom we have a multi-year relationship, where we have advised and provided guidance through multiple stages of their development and on varying corporate and strategic issues.
